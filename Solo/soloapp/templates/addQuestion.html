Writing a good question

A well-crafted question stimulates critical thinking and promotes deeper engagement with a subject matter. By carefully considering the wording and structure of a question, it encourages individuals to analyze and understand the topic from different angles, fostering intellectual growth and expanding one's knowledge base

steps

  • Summarize your problem in a one-line title.
  • Describe your problem in more detail.
  • Describe what you tried and what you expected to happen.
  • Add “tags” which help surface your question to members of the community.
  • Review your question and post it to the site.
